Enrollment FAQs

How long does verification take?

Most professionals are verified within 24-48 hours. During peak times, it may take up to 3 business days.

What if my license is from another state/country?

We verify licenses from all US states and most countries. International professionals welcome! Contact support for specific regions.

Can I hide my rates and discuss them privately?

Yes! You can list general rate ranges publicly or keep rates private. All detailed pricing is discussed directly with clients.

What's your commission/fee structure?

We take 0% commission on direct payments. If using our secure payment system, it's only 2-3%. Complete transparency, no hidden fees.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! No contracts. Cancel anytime with no penalties. Your profile stays active through the end of your billing period.

How do I get more clients/inquiries?

Maintain a great profile, respond quickly to messages, collect reviews, and keep your portfolio updated. Premium members get featured prominently.

Firm Registration FAQs

Who can register?

Contractors, builders, architects, engineers, design studios, and trades. Any construction or design business.

Can we add our team members?

Yes! Invite team members who get accounts with role-based access to company projects.

How do we post projects?

Create detailed project posts with scope, timeline, budget & location. Professionals submit proposals within hours.

Are professionals pre-vetted?

Yes! All professionals are pre-vetted. View ratings, certifications, insurance, and client reviews on their profile.

Is there a minimum project value?

No! From small $500 jobs to $100K+ projects. BuildSmart works at all budget levels.

How does payment work?

Pay directly or use our secure system with milestone-based payments, escrow services for large projects.
